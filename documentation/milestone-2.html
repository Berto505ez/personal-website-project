Content Strategy

Welcome Card:

This portion will introduce myself and my rock-hounding/hunting hobby. It will contain an inviting/encouraging message as well as a description of what this website will contain.

About me:

This component will describe how I got into rock-hounding, how long I've been rock-hounding, where I like to go, and what makes me choose certain rocks over others.

Rock Gallery:

I will display my coolest rocks.

Contact Form:

This element will have a straightforward contact form with a message up top of what possible entries they can submit in the message portion (let me know about my rocks/places to go rock hunting/give me their contact info to go rock hunting together/let me know they like my website etc.).

Wireframes:

My website will have a simple consistent layout that is fully reactive for big and small screens.
