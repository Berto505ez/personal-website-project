About Me

Hello, my name is Tim. I was born and raised here in Albuquerque, New Mexico. I started rock hounding when I was 7 years old. My Uncle Raymond was the first to introduce me to rock hounding. During my adolescence I used rock hounding as my kind of meditation. I made many bad decisions during my teens and one of my only good ones was choosing to go rock hunting. I fell in love with the overwhelming beauty of this states landscape and what's in it. Rock hounding is more than finding some cool rocks. Everything about rock hounding is why I love it, like taking a nice cruise into the wilderness, walking on wild terrain while the sun is on your face, the peaceful silence, letting out a screaming because you can and my favorite of all, scanning the floor you walk on to find some BEAUTIFUL rocks supplied by the land. I know nothing about what kind of rocks I have, and I'm eager to learn so please feel free to educate me about rocks, maybe even recommend a book or two. Thank you for visiting The Rock center, and remember if the rock is too big for your car, get a truck!
